About LaunchPy

Helping software engineers transition to high-impact AI careers

Our Story

LaunchPy was founded in 2023 by a team of AI practitioners and educators who saw a critical gap in the industry: While demand for AI engineers was skyrocketing, there weren't enough specialized programs to help experienced software engineers make the transition.

As former software engineers ourselves who successfully pivoted into AI careers, we understood the unique challenges faced by developers looking to enter this field. Traditional CS programs were too theoretical and generalized, while most bootcamps lacked the technical depth required for true AI expertise.

We built LaunchPy to be the program we wished we had when making our own career transitions—focused, practical, and specifically designed for experienced software developers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower software engineers to become skilled AI practitioners who can build responsible, effective AI systems that solve real-world problems.

Practical Expertise

We focus on hands-on, industry-relevant skills over theoretical knowledge alone.

Community Support

We build strong learning communities where engineers help each other succeed.

Career Advancement

We prioritize outcomes that lead to tangible career progression and opportunities.
